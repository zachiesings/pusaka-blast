# Pusaka Blast vs Pusaka Tiles — Guideline 4.3(a) differentiation

Both apps ship from the same App Store team. This documents how **Pusaka Blast**
is unmistakably its own app, distinct from **Pusaka Tiles**.

| Dimension | Pusaka Blast | Pusaka Tiles |
|---|---|---|
| **Genre / mechanic** | Batik **block-elimination puzzle** — place blocks on an 8×8 board, clear full rows/columns | **Rhythm / music game** — tap falling tiles to play melodies |
| **Mascot** | **Macan Batik** — a chubby, grounded **batik tiger cub** (warm amber/sogan fur, kawung stripes, paws, tail) | **Roh Gamelan** — a tall, **hovering temple-dancer spirit** (indigo/violet robe, wayang crown, no feet) |
| **Display font** | **Cinzel** (regal Roman **serif**) | **Outfit** (geometric **sans-serif**) |
| **Palette** | Warm **keraton/batik** — sogan brown, prada gold, maroon, terracotta, olive | Cool **temple night** — indigo, violet, teal, pink, cyan |
| **Tile/board look** | Warm batik tiles with a real **kawung** motif + prada-gold rim, on a sogan board | Luminous glassy indigo lane-tiles falling on a night stage |
| **Signature moment** | **Berkah Keraton** — gold particle storm + gong (×2 scoring) | **FEVER** — pink/violet storm + beat pulse (×2 scoring) |
| **Layouts** | Block board + tray + power-up bar; Petualangan rope-map; batik skin shop | 4-lane falling-tile board; song-select album cards; lane-theme shop |
| **App Store metadata** | "puzzle balok batik keraton" — block puzzle keywords, no rhythm wording | "game ketuk ubin / rhythm" — rhythm keywords, no puzzle wording |
| **Bundle id** | id.pusakablast.pusakablast | id.pusakatiles.pusakatiles |

Both are code-driven (Flutter CustomPainter/particles), license-clear assets only
(OFL fonts, FluidR3_GM SoundFont, original motifs/audio). No shared mascot, font,
palette, layout, or listing copy.
